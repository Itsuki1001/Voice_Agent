Title: Headphones Not Connecting
Type: troubleshooting

Problem:
User is unable to connect headphones via Bluetooth

Solution:
1. Turn off Bluetooth and turn it back on
2. Reset headphones (hold power button for 10 seconds)
3. Remove device from paired list and reconnect
4. Ensure device is charged

Keywords:
bluetooth issue, pairing problem
